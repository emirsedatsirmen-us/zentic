Engineering Excellence

Built for the Modern Web. Engineered to Scale.

A powerful interface is useless without a revolutionary backend engine. Zentic completely discards the legacy REST API architectures that plague traditional communication apps. By leveraging persistent, bi-directional WebSocket connections directly tied to a highly distributed NoSQL cloud database, we have effectively eliminated platform latency.

  • Serverless Edge Infrastructure Your data is routed through the closest global edge node, ensuring sub-50ms ping times globally.
  • Cryptographic Security Rules Private server data is locked at the database tier. It is mathematically impossible for unauthorized users to query your community's data.
  • Client-Side Media Compression High-resolution images are intelligently compressed directly in your browser before upload, saving massive bandwidth and drastically improving load times.

Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about migrating your community to the Zentic Network.

How hard is it to migrate my existing Discord server to Zentic?
Migration is incredibly seamless. You can create a fully functional Zentic private server in under 5 seconds. From there, simply generate a secure invite link, pin it in your old Discord server, and watch your community transition to a faster, cleaner environment.
Do my members need to download a heavy application?
No. This is one of Zentic's massive architectural advantages. Zentic is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Your members simply click the invite link and the platform loads instantly within their native browser (Safari, Chrome, Firefox) on both desktop and mobile, with full OS-level notification support.
What makes Zentic more secure than other communication platforms?
We utilize strict database-level isolation. If your server is set to private, its data cannot be indexed or queried by unauthorized users—even other authenticated members of the global network. Furthermore, we rely on enterprise-grade JWT authentication and refuse to implement any third-party advertising trackers that compromise your digital footprint.
Is there a limit to how many members my private server can have?
Zentic's NoSQL backend and edge-routing architecture are designed to handle massive concurrency. Whether you have 10 enterprise team members or a Web3 DAO with 50,000 active participants, the infrastructure automatically scales without degradation in chat latency or timeline rendering.
What is the difference between Timeline Channels and Chat Channels?
Chat Channels operate exactly like Slack or Discord—rapid, real-time, ephemeral conversation streams. Timeline Channels operate more like a highly curated micro-blog or forum. They are designed for long-form updates, announcements, and deep threaded discussions that shouldn't get buried in a fast-moving chat room.
