1. The Secure Registration Process

Authentication

Legacy social networks weaponize the registration process. They mandate phone numbers to bypass multi-factor authentication, only to subsequently sell those numbers to data brokers or use them to build invasive cross-platform advertising profiles. Zentic fundamentally rejects this model.

Email-Only Verification

Zentic requires only a valid email address to establish an identity. We utilize industry-standard cryptographic hashing protocols via Google Firebase Authentication. Your password is encrypted instantly on your device and transmitted as an irreversible hash; no plain-text passwords ever touch our NoSQL databases.

  • Zero Phone Number Tracking: We will never ask for your mobile phone number. Your physical identity remains detached from your digital persona.
  • Encrypted JWT Sessions: Upon successful registration, your browser receives a securely signed JSON Web Token (JWT). This token handles subsequent authentication, ensuring your session cannot be hijacked by malicious actors intercepting network traffic.

2. Anonymous "Read-Only" Browsing

A true commitment to privacy means allowing users to interact with public data without sacrificing their anonymity. Zentic is one of the only premium social networks to feature a robust Anonymous Guest Mode.

When you click the "Open Zentic" link without logging in, the application seamlessly triggers an anonymous authentication flow. The system generates a temporary, cryptographically secure session ID that contains absolutely no personally identifiable information (PII).

The Guest Experience

While operating in Anonymous Mode, you can scroll the Global Timeline, search for specific hashtags, read long-form posts, and view public community directories. The system only prompts you to securely register if you attempt to write data to the servers (e.g., leaving a comment, liking a post, or joining a private community). This provides a frictionless, zero-commitment onboarding experience.

3. The Zero Data Harvesting Policy

If you are not paying for the product, you are the product. Because Zentic operates on a premium, community-driven ecosystem rather than an advertising-driven model, we have completely decoupled our business from the surveillance economy.

We do not run advertisements. Therefore, we do not need your data.

  • No Third-Party Trackers: The Zentic application does not deploy Meta Pixels, Google Analytics Advertising IDs, or hidden tracking scripts.
  • No Cross-Site Surveillance: We do not track the websites you visit outside of the Zentic ecosystem. Your browser history is yours alone.
  • No Algorithmic Profiling: Because our timeline ranking (the Hotness Score) is based on transparent, organic mathematics, we do not build hidden psychological profiles to feed you addictive content.

4. GDPR, CCPA & The Right to be Forgotten

Zentic was architected from day one to exceed the rigorous requirements of the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Enterprise B2B clients and individual consumers alike require absolute control over their digital sovereignty.

Instant Account Erasure

Many legacy platforms make it intentionally difficult to delete an account, hiding the option behind labyrinthine menus and imposing 30-day "cooling off" periods where your data remains active. Zentic honors the Right to be Forgotten implicitly.

When a user initiates an account deletion via the settings panel, the platform triggers an irreversible cloud function. Your authentication records, your personalized settings, your direct messages, and your timeline posts are systematically expunged from the active Firestore database and all associated edge nodes. Once the command executes, the data cannot be recovered. You own your footprint.
