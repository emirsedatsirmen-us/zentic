2. Platform Compliance & Digital Trust

Zentic operates on the bleeding edge of the premium social networking industry. To maintain our status as the world's most secure, ad-free alternative to legacy platforms like Discord and Slack, we adhere to strict global compliance frameworks.

Data Sovereignty & GDPR

We believe that your data belongs exclusively to you. Zentic is engineered with fundamental adherence to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We do not aggregate user demographics to sell to third-party advertising brokers. Our architecture strictly isolates multi-tenant databases, ensuring that private community servers remain impenetrable to unauthorized data scraping.

Content Moderation & Liability

Zentic empowers community server owners with supreme moderation privileges. While we provide the underlying NoSQL architecture, server administrators are responsible for maintaining the decorum of their own spaces. Our automated client-side filters proactively sanitize universally recognized malicious content, mitigating platform liability and preserving network integrity.
